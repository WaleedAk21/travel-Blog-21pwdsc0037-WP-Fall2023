Burj Khalifa

Explore the iconic Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest skyscraper. Visit the observation deck for breathtaking panoramic views of Dubai.

You can easily get to Burj Khalifa using the following public transportation options:

Domestic travelers:

  • Dubai Metro:

    • The metros Red Line has a dedicated Burj Khalifa station. Once you are on the Red Line, get off at the Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall stop, which is within walking distance of the tower. From a popular area like Palm Jumeirah, the shortest journey to Burj Khalifa includes taking the Palm Monorail from your closest station on the island to the Palm Gateway; from here, walk or take a taxi to the Al Khail Metro Station and get on the Red Line heading towards Centrepoint.

  • Bus:

    • Routes closest to Burj Khalifa include 27, 28, 29, 50 and 81, which stop at Dubai Mall. From a popular site like Global Village, the shortest journey to Burj Khalifa includes taking buses 104, C26 and 28 from start to finish. Please visit rta.ae for detailed public transportation schedules.

International travelers


  1. Travel Documents:
    2. Ensure you have the necessary travel documents to enter Dubai, which typically include a passport with a valid visa. Check the specific visa requirements for your country on the official website of the UAE Embassy or consulate.
  2. Flight Booking:
    3. Book a flight to Dubai through a reputable airline. Dubai International Airport (DXB) is the main airport in the city, and there are also some other options, like Al Maktoum International Airport (DWC).
  3. Accommodation:
    4. Book accommodation in Dubai. There are many hotels and resorts to choose from, and some of them are in close proximity to the Burj Khalifa.
  4. Buy Tickets:
    5. Purchase tickets to visit the Burj Khalifa. You can buy tickets online in advance through the official burjkhalifa website or at the ticket counter in Dubai Mall. It's highly recommended to book tickets in advance, especially if you plan to visit during peak tourist seasons.
  5. Transportation:
    6. You can use public transportation, taxis, or rideshare services to reach the Burj Khalifa. The Dubai Metro is an efficient way to travel around the city, and there is a station nearby called the Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall station.
  6. Visit Time:
    7. Plan your visit during your preferred time, whether during the day or in the evening to witness the stunning cityscape.
  7. Enjoy Your Visit:
    8. Take in the breathtaking views of Dubai from the observation deck. There are telescopes, informative displays, and photo opportunities.






Palm Jumeirah

Relax on the beautiful Palm Jumeirah, an artificial palm-shaped island, with luxurious resorts and pristine beaches.

Domestic travelers:

  • Dubai Metro:

    • Use the Dubai Metro Red Line and get off at the Dubai Internet City or Al Khail Metro Station. From here, you can either take a taxi or bus.

  • Bus:

    • Look for the bus stop near either of the metro stations mentioned above and check the route map to find the bus that serves Palm Jumeirah. Bus routes like D04, F37 and F41 are a few options. Please visit rta.ae for detailed public transportation schedules.
